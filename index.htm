BOHEMIA

Bohemia is famous Punjabi rapper, artist. Roger David (Punjabi: راجر ڈیوڈ, pronounced [ɾaːd͡ʒəɾ ɖeːʋɪɖ]; born 15 October 1979), better known by his stage name Bohemia (pronounced [boːɦiːmiːaː]), is a Pakistani-American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer, mainly associated with Punjabi language music industry.He is considered to be the pioneer of the Desi hip hop industry.   Source
